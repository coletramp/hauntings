The softball closet in Cowles Gym.

Location: Cowles Gym

Paranormal Entity: Unknown
Cowles Gym seems to be haunted by many different entities. Our hunters have preformed multiple overnight investigations in the gym and discovered several anomalous occurences. Various objects have been observed to be mysteriously thrown out the softball closet. Screams, banging on doors, and lights flickering has also been reported.

Our hunters documented the objects thrown out of the closet on the first night.
