The Haunting of College Hall

College Hall Haunting:

College Hall is haunted by a former student named Mildred Hedges. Mildred was a freshman home economics major, who caught her heel on the stairs and fell to her death from the third floor. Students say they see her peering through the windows of College Hall.

Many students do not take these stories seriously, please take caution in these buildings!


Please do not assume this is a myth!
